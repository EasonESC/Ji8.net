Ji8.net - About J8 and 8i1 and Ji8.net
1.  JASHO.ORG, along with its subsidiaries Ji8.net, Pi8.net, and Ci8.net, founded on 1997-07-01, are integrated organizations dedicated to the frontier of advanced technologies. Through continuous efforts in developing new technologies, the mission of J8, Ji8.net, Pi8.net, and Ci8.net is to provide superb solutions to communication operators and technology-managed entities. Ji8.net specializes in carrier traffic exchange and network communication and is a communication carrier and operator in the United States of America and other parts of the world. 
2.  As the organization grows, Ji8.net (as of 1997-07-01) invests in or operates businesses in the fields of finance, banking, funding, accounting, management, legal, channel management, communication, system integration, and advanced technology research and development. Ji8.net currently owns and operates businesses in the United States of America, China, Taiwan, and Hong Kong. 
Ji8.net - Organization Culture
3.  The operation principle of Ji8.net is “Detail is the key to success,” so we must fully execute the Standard Operation Procedure with the principle of strict self-discipline. Small mistakes accumulate into the failure of a company, while small gains accumulate into the achievement of a company. Strict self-discipline is essential for a successful company, so members of Ji8.net must constantly study and push themselves to go beyond their limits. Ji8.net will continually invest in fully automated management, systems for acceptance checks, and transparency of organizational information to improve. This ensures our group members can better contribute to helping Ji8.net gain a competitive advantage and achieve its goals. 
4.  Our company believes that all knowledge and skills can be gained through learning. As long as you work hard to push yourself, you can achieve your goals. 
6.  All members of Ji8.net must adhere to the basic rule, the “Organization Management Manual,” which includes acceptance checks. If a rule is found to be incorrect, the Organization Management Manual must be amended accordingly. All members must follow the updated rule after it is verified as correct. Members of Ji8.net possess self-judgment and independent working abilities. Each task is treated as a project, which must have a clearly defined target, summary, standard, budget date, and budget expenditure. Once a target is set, it must be completed. In short, a project cannot be abandoned halfway, and its priority depends on its benefit to the company. 
7.  All details and information must be recorded and uploaded, including searches, integrations, selections, and suggestions. All issue resolutions and execution conditions must be documented, and system technology must be continuously optimized.
